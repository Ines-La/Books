SURS Description

The university’s dean decided to lessen the pressure on the restaurant staff, reduce the queue size and organize and optimize the process of passing through ticket control. All this shall be done through a QR code now. Every student/teacher will receive a card with a unique QR code containing personal data when they register. The new SURS will allow the following functionalities: to check if the QR code is included in the database, to make automatic incrementing in the number of meals eaten each time the QR code is scanned, to send a bill that contains the total number of meals, the total amount to be paid, the deadline, and the account number that you need to send money to, at the end of each month, and to refuse access to a specific QR code if its owner did not pay the fees of one month. The restaurant managers may use the SURS to check payments each period and to update the database. The SURS system is intended to manage the limited time of lunch by allowing the targeted audience to move in a faster way and lessen the burden on the staff. The SURS allows the members to pay with their credit/debit cards by transferring money from their personal accounts to the restaurant account each time they receive a bill via e-mails.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you just have to inform the restaurant manager to cancel your card.

Is the monthly fee required?

Yes, because the system will refuse access to a specific QR code if its owner did not pay the fees of one month.

In case the QR code card is lost, what shoud I do?

You have to inform the restaurant manager so you can have a new card and cancel the old one to avoid the risk that it can be used by someone else.

Can I have a double dish?

Yes, there is no lmited number of dishs. The system makes automatic incrementing in the number of meals eaten each time the QR code is scanned.
